06 / FAQ Before you buy

Questions,
answered.

01 What does CANxiety mean?

CANxiety is built on the idea that even when anxiety, pressure or doubt makes you feel like you can't, you still CAN. C.A.N means Challenge Any Negativity.

02 Why “Challenge Any Negativity”?

Because the brand is not about pretending negativity disappears. It is about challenging the thought, the pressure and the voice that says stop. Keep challenging it, and over time you begin to conquer what tried to stop you.

03 Is CANxiety a mental health charity?

No. CANxiety is a clothing and identity brand, not a charity or medical service. The message is about self-belief, pressure, movement and expression through clothing.

04 Does CANxiety claim to treat anxiety?

No. CANxiety Clothing does not provide medical advice, diagnosis or mental health treatment. If you need support, contact a qualified healthcare professional or an appropriate support service.
