05 / Why The meaning beneath

Anxiety is often invisible. CANxiety makes it wearable.

Not as a label, but as a statement of control, strength, and identity. The name holds the tension between “CAN” and “anxiety”: the pressure is real, and movement is still possible.

CANxiety is an independent fashion and identity brand. It never glamorises struggle and does not claim clothing can diagnose, cure, or treat anxiety.

08 / FAQ Before the signal

Questions,
answered.

01 What is CANxiety?

CANxiety Clothing is an independent UK streetwear and fashion identity brand. It turns themes of pressure, survival, confidence and self-expression into limited clothing and visual culture.

02 When is the first drop?

Drop 001 is in its founder-list phase. Reveal, reservation and release dates will be shared with founder members first.

03 Can I pre-order?

Pre-drop reservations will open in a limited window. Before checkout opens, Reserve now carries your chosen item into the founder-list request.

04 Are items limited?

Yes. The first release is planned as a limited run, and restocks are not promised. Final quantities will be confirmed before reservations open.

05 How do payments work?

Payments will be processed away from this website through a trusted provider such as Stripe, Shopify or PayPal. CANxiety never asks for card details on this static site.

06 What if my size sells out?

Founder members receive priority sizing notifications. Joining early gives you the best chance, but no size is guaranteed until checkout is completed.

07 Do you ship across the UK?

UK-wide shipping is planned. Final delivery areas, costs and estimated dispatch dates will be confirmed before payment.

08 Is this a mental health charity?

No. CANxiety is a clothing and identity brand. Any future charity partnership will be described clearly and separately.

09 Does CANxiety claim to treat anxiety?

No. CANxiety Clothing does not provide medical advice, diagnosis or mental health treatment. If you need support, contact a qualified healthcare professional or an appropriate support service.
